# Coursing

You can use courses to help with meal pacing in your restaurant.

## How to setup coursing

First step is to create courses:

<img src="/assets/table-service/add-course.png" alt="Add course" class="support-image">

Then give your course a <b>Title</b>, select a Store <b>Location</b> for it to be associated with and click <b>Create</b>:

<img src="/assets/table-service/create-course.png" alt="Create course" class="support-image">

A new tab will open for the created course, allowing you to associate products with the course. <br/>This is optional, doing so will make sure this course is automatically selected for the associated product in Simmer POS:

<img src="/assets/browse-modifier-products.png" alt="Browse products" class="support-image">

## How to use coursing

Once your courses has been created, you can use them in the Simmer POS extension when adding a product to a ticket.

<img src="/assets/table-service/pos-product-course.png" alt="POS product course" class="support-image">

You can also view and edit the line item course via the "Ticket" > "Edit Line Item" screen:

<img src="/assets/table-service/pos-ticket-courses.png" alt="POS ticket courses" class="support-image">

<img src="/assets/table-service/pos-edit-item-courses.png" alt="POS line item courses" class="support-image">

<b>Note -</b> Ensure you click save after making changes to the line item.

### Sending courses

When sending tickets to your KDS or printers from the POS you will now have the option to select the course you would like to send:

<img src="/assets/table-service/pos-send-courses.png" alt="POS send courses" class="support-image">

Only the items that are in the sent courses will be displayed on the associated KDS or printed tickets, with their course clearly labelled:

<img src="/assets/table-service/kds-coursing.png" alt="KDS coursing" class="support-image">
